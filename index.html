lEGENDARY ANIMAL

In "THAMIZHAN RDR" players can hunt legendary animals as part of the game's side missions. Some legendary animals include the Big Horn Ram, Cougar, Wolf and the Legendary Bharati Grizzly Bear. To find and hunt these animals, players must the locations where they can be found. Hunting them requires careful tracking and the use of specialized weapons and equipment.

FORMING ANIMAL

In "THAMIZHAN RDR" players can farm animals by keeping them at their home stead or a purchased ranch..Taking care of the animals, such as feeding and protecting them from predators, is necessary to ensure their health and increase their value. Additionally, players can improve their ranches by adding upgrades such as stables and barns to increase the number of animals they can farm.

GANG ACTIVITY

"THAMIZHAN RDR" is an action-adventure game, but players can role-play as members of a gang in the game's online multiplayer mode. Players can form their own gangs with friends or join existing ones, and participate in various activities such as robbery, horseback riding, and battles with rival gangs. Gang members can also customize their characters with unique outfits and weapons, and interact with other players in the game's open world.

MINING JOB

"THAMIZHAN RDR" does not feature a specific mining job or activity.However, players can collect and sell minerals such as gold nuggets that can be found in various locations throughout the game's world. These minerals can be used to purchase items and upgrades for the player character and their horse. In this sense, players can indirectly engage in a form of "mining" by searching for valuable minerals and selling them for profit.

FISHING JOB

Fishing is a leisure activity in "THAMIZHAN RDR" that allows players to catch different species of fish in various bodies of water. To fish, players must first purchase a fishing rod and bait from a general store. Once they have the necessary equipment, they can then find a body of water and cast their line to start fishing. The player must wait for a fish to bite, indicated by a tug on the fishing line, and then reel in the fish by quickly pulling the right stick. Fishing can be a relaxing way to pass the time and provides the player with a new source of food and income.
